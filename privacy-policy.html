Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2025


At Next Level Complete Auto Care Center LLC, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the personal information you provide to us through our website and communications.

Information We Collect

When you use our website or contact us, we may collect personal information including your name, phone number, email address, and any message or form submission details. We may also collect technical data such as IP addresses and browser information to improve site functionality and security.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to respond to your inquiries, provide service updates and appointment confirmations, send promotional offers (if you opt in), and improve our services and user experience. We do not share your mobile information with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties, except for aggregators and providers of the text message services who help us deliver the SMS messages.

Your Communication Choices

By submitting your phone number through our forms, you agree to receive SMS messages from us. Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. You can reply “STOP” at any time to unsubscribe or “HELP” for assistance.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with unrelated third parties. In some cases, we may share data with trusted vendors and service providers who help us operate our website or send communications — but only to the extent necessary and never for their own marketing purposes. All use case categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties, excluding aggregators and providers of the Text Message services.

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: info@nextlevelcompleteauto.com or call (865) 350-2700 .
