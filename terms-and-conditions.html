Opt-Out Instructions

You can cancel the SMS service at any time. Simply text “STOP” to the number from which you received messages. Once we receive your “STOP” message, we will confirm your unsubscribe status via SMS. After that, you will no longer receive SMS messages from us.

If you wish to rejoin our SMS program after opting out, you may sign up again through our website or send a new opt-in request as you did initially. Upon confirming your new subscription, we will resume sending SMS messages.
